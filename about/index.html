Growing up, I always knew I wanted to be a designer. My mother was a designer. It is in my blood. Right out of highschool I went to art school at College for Creative studies where I initially majored in advertising and minored in photography. After a year, I switched my major to Graphic Design so I could be more experimental and changed my minor to printmaking. My passion bloomed from there and I quickly became obsessed with all things relating to typography, pattern and illustration. After graduating with a bachlor degree in Graphic Design and media arts, I expanded my education at skillcrush to be more foused on web design.
